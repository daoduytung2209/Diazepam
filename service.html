• Delivery details

    • All our products are delivered within 4 working days of the order being placed. For orders placed on weekends or public holidays an additional delivery period of 2 days can be expected despite our best efforts to deliver your orders on time.

  • Free Delivery

    • For every order cost $50 or more we will deliver it at your door step free of charge (not available for international orders). For all local deliveries below $50 a flat delivery charge of $7 would be charged. For international deliveries, standard courier charges apply.

  • Happy Hours

    • From 3pm-5pm every Monday, you can get an extra coffee for every ice cream you choose! For children from 5 to 9, can come to Diazepam coffee at every Sunday of the month at 8pm to get free ice and receive voucher from our shop

  • Cash on Delivery

    • Cash on Delivery is only available for orders less than 3 items. For larger orders we would prefer to first make payment on web before the order is collected and the product is dispatched.
