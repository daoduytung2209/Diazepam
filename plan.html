Mission Statement

The website is intended to promote Diazepam Cafe, an Ice Cream Cafe shop, by providing their ice cream products, events and services to customers. The website is also intended to provide a simple and easy interface for customers to place orders and make payments.

Success Evaluation

The number of people who come to Diazepam Cafe's events and make purchases at Diazepam Cafe shop is expected to increase by atleast 30%. By making people aware of the products available at Diazepam Cafe shop more people are likely to place orders and purchase ice cream.

Target Audience

Our main audience will be children and youngster who love ice cream the most. Our website will be design with bright colors, good-looking pictures and effective architecture to attach children and youngster.
