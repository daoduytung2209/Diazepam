Mission Statement

The website is intended to promote Diazepam, an Ice Cream Cafe shop, by providing their ice cream products, events and services to customers.

Success Evaluation

After 2 months getting the website, the numbers of customers coming to Diazepam to join events and purchase the products will be counted and compared to the previous 2 months. The sales productivity is also expected to increase by at least 30%.

Target Audience

Our main audience will be children and youngster who are ice-cream lovers. Our website will be design with bright colors, good-looking pictures and effective architecture to attach children and youngster.
